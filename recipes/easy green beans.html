Easy Garden Green Beans

Description

Delisciously steamed and parmesian tossed green beans are not only amazing but flavorful

something that taste so good shouldnt be this healthy

but here we are green beans steamed to the perfect crunch and tossed in parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 1lb fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 3tbls olive olive oil
  • 3tbls freshly grated parmesan
  • 2tbls white wine vinegar
  • 3 clove garlic,sliced
  • 1 pink salt
  • 1 pink ground black peper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. gather all ingredients
  2. steam veggies in saucepan or steamer with just water and bring to a boil for 5 minutes
  3. transfer steamed veggies into a bowl add olive oil,parm,white wine vinagar,garlic,salt and pepper then toss and sit for 10 minutes
  4. remove garlic slices and serve
