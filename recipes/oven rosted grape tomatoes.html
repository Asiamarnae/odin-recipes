Oven Roasted Grape Tomatoes

A healthy alternative to your everyday side dishes that can upscale any dinner party.

This aromatic,and savory dish will be the perfect pairing for any event or get together.

The garlic and basial paired with Robust tomatoes make an elegant side dish and maybe even to eat as the main course.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound grape tomatoes,halved
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 fresh basil leaves,chopped
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. preheat over to 350 degree F(175 degrees C)
  2. Place tomatoes onto a large square of aluminum foil. Drizzle olive oil over tomatoes and top with garlic, basil, thyme, and salt. Wrap the foil around tomato mixture sealing tightly to keep juices inside.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until tomatoes are tender, about 30 minutes. Cool slightly.
